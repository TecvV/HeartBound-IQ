## 🎼 Core Agent System

> The heart of HeartBound IQ — a multi-agent workflow that plans your entire wedding from a single brief.

| Agent | Role |
|---|---|
| 🎼 **Orchestrator Agent** | Runs the full workflow, auto-picks top vendors, and keeps selections, confirmations, and budgets in sync across every agent |
| 🏛️ **Venue Scout Agent** | Surfaces top venues with AI-ranked picks — pricing, capacity, ratings, and amenities scored and ready |
| 🍽️ **Catering Agent** | Compares menus, cuisines, and packages matched to your guest count and budget |
| 📸 **Vendor Agent** | AI-scored picks across photographers, decorators, DJs, and more — category-wise with transparent scoring |
| 📅 **Timeline Agent** | Auto-generates a complete day-of schedule and key planning milestones |
| 👥 **Guest Agent** | Manages your full guest list, RSVPs, dietary preferences, and invitation scope |

## 🛠️ Planning & Management Features

### Decision Intelligence
- **Decision Review Dashboard** — Compares AI recommendations vs your selections side-by-side, highlighting every difference so you always know what you're choosing and why
- **Compare Mode** — Side-by-side vendor and venue comparisons for confident, informed decisions — no more spreadsheets
- **Gated Tools Workflow** — Advanced steps unlock only after required confirmations are complete, guiding you through the right sequence at every stage

### Budget & Finance
- **Budget Tracker** — Expected totals, paid/unpaid tracking, and category-wise expense management — every cost accounted for, always up to date

## 👥 Guest & Invitation Suite

- **Guest Management System** — Full guest list with RSVP status, dietary preferences, and per-guest invitation scope (guest only vs guest + family)
- **RSVP System** — A unique public RSVP link per guest that updates the main list automatically — no manual follow-up
- **Invitation Card Designer** — Multiple themes, live preview, and PDF generation
- **Invite-All Workflow** — Sends the finalized invitation card PDF and wedding details to every guest in one action
- **Wedding Website Generator** — Auto-fills from confirmed vendors and finalized selections; export as a shareable PDF or live site
- **Exportable Wedding Plan PDF** — Complete downloadable plan for sharing, printing, and records

## ✨ Advanced AI Enhancements

> Cutting-edge capabilities that set HeartBound IQ apart.

### 📱 WhatsApp Invitation Delivery
Send finalized invitations via **WhatsApp Business API** — high open-rate delivery with RSVP replies inside WhatsApp that instantly update your guest list.

### 🎙️ AI Voice Agent
Autonomously contacts vendors, logs conversations, extracts key details, and feeds insights directly into vendor scoring and recommendations.

### 🔍 Vendor Review Sentiment Analyzer
Reads top reviews and surfaces concrete insights — repeated service issues or praised strengths — going far beyond star ratings.

### 🌦️ Weather-Aware Timeline
Checks the wedding date forecast and auto-adds contingency steps for outdoor events — no surprises on the day.

### 🌏 Multi-City Comparison Agent
Built for destination weddings — runs parallel plans across multiple cities with cost and quality comparisons side by side.

### 📷 AI Post-Wedding Photo Delivery
Matches guest faces from the wedding gallery and auto-sends each guest their photos via email and WhatsApp — zero manual sorting.
